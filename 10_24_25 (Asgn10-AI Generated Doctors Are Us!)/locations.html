Our Locations

We serve patients across the United States. Below are a few of our locations.

Lynchburg, VA (Headquarters)

2334 South West Ave., Lynchburg, VA 24502

Our flagship location and administrative headquarters, the Lynchburg clinic offers comprehensive family medicine, same-day appointments, and telehealth support. It’s home to our leadership team and training center for new clinicians.

Richmond, VA

134 Medical Plaza Dr., Richmond, VA

Conveniently located in the heart of Richmond, this clinic specializes in preventative care, women’s health, and chronic condition management. The facility also features extended evening hours for working professionals and families.

Charlotte, NC

89 Healthway Blvd., Charlotte, NC

Our Charlotte location offers a full range of outpatient services, including pediatric care, immunizations, and diagnostic testing. Patients appreciate its welcoming staff, convenient parking, and close proximity to major highways.

Chicago, IL

210 Lakeview Ave., Chicago, IL

Situated near downtown, the Chicago clinic focuses on urgent care, occupational health, and walk-in services. Its modern facility includes on-site labs and imaging to provide patients with quick, comprehensive care.
