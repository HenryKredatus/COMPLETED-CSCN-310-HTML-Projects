About Doctors Are Us!

Our History

Founded to bring quality, accessible healthcare to communities nationwide, Doctors Are Us! began as a single practice in Lynchburg, Virginia, with a simple idea: to deliver dependable care with genuine compassion. Over the years, our commitment to putting patients first has allowed us to grow into a trusted network of clinics across multiple states. From the start, we have embraced technology and modern medicine to improve convenience and outcomes for patients. With innovations like virtual visits and same-day appointments, we continue to adapt to the needs of today’s families — ensuring care is always within reach, whether you’re at home, at work, or on the go.

Mission

To make healthcare compassionate, convenient, and affordable for everyone. We believe that every patient deserves access to high-quality medical attention without long waits, confusing processes, or high costs. Our goal is to simplify the path to better health by offering flexible care options, transparent pricing, and a patient-first approach at every level of service. Through community outreach, modern technology, and an exceptional team of healthcare professionals, Doctors Are Us! strives to build lasting relationships with every patient. Together, we’re redefining what healthcare can be — personal, proactive, and focused on you.

Our clinics are designed with patient comfort in mind.
