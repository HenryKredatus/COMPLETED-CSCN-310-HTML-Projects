Campus Life

Studying on Mars is a truly out of this world experience! It might even take your breath away! All main campus areas are enclosed in large biodomes, but space suits are obviously required when leaving the premises. Water and food are regularly brought via supply shipments. Tenures at MU have an approximately 53.7% survival rate, so you've got nothing to worry about! We didn't call this page campus LIFE for nothing!
