Admissions

So first you hop on a rocket ship. Then you hope you survive the 6-12 month space flight. Then you land and hope you don't immediately get eaten by Marvin the Martian. And you if survive that, it's all smooth sailing! Good luck actually sailing though, without water. To apply, simply speak to one of our many reptilian attendants found around campus who will walk you through the process! Once accepted, you will be living in one of our modular dorms (good luck commuting from home...) and getting around with our cutting-edge system of trams and tunnels.
