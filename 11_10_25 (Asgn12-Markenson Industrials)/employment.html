Employment Opportunities

Jobs Available

  1. Senior Mechanical Engineer – Lead the design and development of mechanical systems, ensuring efficiency and reliability.
  2. Control Systems Technician – Install, maintain, and troubleshoot automated control systems in production environments.
  3. Fabrication Supervisor – Oversee fabrication processes, manage the team, and ensure quality and safety standards are met.
  4. Logistics Coordinator – Manage supply chain operations, track shipments, and optimize inventory management.
  5. Quality Assurance Inspector – Conduct inspections and tests to ensure products meet all regulatory and company standards.
  6. Research & Development Specialist – Work on innovative projects, prototype new products, and support continuous improvement initiatives.
  7. Environmental Health & Safety Officer – Implement safety protocols, monitor compliance, and provide staff training.
  8. Technical Writer – Prepare manuals, documentation, and process guides for internal and external stakeholders.

Benefits

Competitive pay, health benefits, 401(k) retirement plan, paid time off, and professional development programs. Additional perks include:

  • Flexible work schedules and remote work options
  • Employee wellness programs and gym membership discounts
  • Opportunities for career growth and internal promotions
  • Paid training, certifications, and continuing education support
  • Team-building events and company-sponsored social activities
