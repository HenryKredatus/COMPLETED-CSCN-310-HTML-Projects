Services

What We Offer

Markenson Industrials provides a full spectrum of engineering, manufacturing, and operational support services to clients across multiple sectors. Our expertise allows us to design and deliver reliable systems that meet the specific needs of each industry we serve.

  • Custom Manufacturing & Fabrication — We specialize in precision metalwork, equipment fabrication, and component assembly tailored to meet demanding industrial specifications. Our facilities are equipped to handle both large-scale production runs and custom prototype development, ensuring quality from concept to completion.
  • Industrial Automation & Controls — Our automation engineers design and implement advanced control systems that enhance efficiency, safety, and performance across multiple industries. From PLC programming to full-scale robotic integration, we help clients modernize their operations and reduce downtime through intelligent automation solutions.
  • Logistics & Supply Chain Solutions — We coordinate end-to-end supply chain services, including procurement, materials handling, and distribution logistics. Our systems are built to support just-in-time manufacturing and global sourcing strategies, reducing lead times and improving inventory management.
  • Maintenance & Field Services — Our certified technicians provide on-site equipment maintenance, emergency repair, and performance optimization. Whether it’s preventive care or rapid response, we ensure industrial systems operate safely and efficiently under demanding conditions.

Industries We Serve

With decades of cross-sector experience, Markenson Industrials supports a diverse range of industries, providing specialized knowledge and adaptable solutions for every client.

  • Energy & Utilities — We design and maintain high-efficiency systems for power generation, renewable energy, and transmission infrastructure, ensuring reliable and sustainable operations.
  • Transportation & Automotive — Our precision components and assembly systems support manufacturers in the automotive, rail, and heavy transport sectors, focusing on safety, durability, and innovation.
  • Food & Beverage Processing — We provide stainless-steel fabrication, automated packaging solutions, and sanitary production systems designed to meet FDA and USDA standards.
  • Construction & Materials — From heavy equipment fabrication to bulk materials handling, we serve construction suppliers and materials producers with rugged, high-capacity solutions.
