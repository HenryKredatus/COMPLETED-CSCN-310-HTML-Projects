About Markenson Industrials

History

Founded in 1974, Markenson Industrials began as a small, family-operated fabrication workshop in Samson, Arizona, serving local agricultural and construction businesses. Over the decades, the company evolved from a regional metalworks supplier into a full-scale industrial solutions provider, offering precision manufacturing, automation integration, and logistics coordination for clients nationwide.

In the 1980s, Markenson expanded into custom machinery design, building its first proprietary assembly systems that improved output and reduced maintenance costs for heavy equipment manufacturers. By the 1990s, continued investment in automation and CNC technologies allowed the company to handle larger contracts and meet strict international production standards.

The early 2000s marked a turning point, as Markenson Industrials partnered with energy and transportation companies to develop more efficient components and systems aimed at reducing environmental impact. This era also saw the construction of a new 85,000-square-foot headquarters and production facility in Samson, designed for both innovation and sustainability.

Today, Markenson Industrials stands as a trusted name in American manufacturing — combining over five decades of experience with a commitment to modernization, employee development, and environmental responsibility. The company continues to expand its research initiatives, focusing on automation, materials science, and green manufacturing to meet the challenges of the next generation of industry.

Mission and Values

Our Mission

At Markenson Industrials, our mission is to deliver reliable, high-quality industrial solutions that empower businesses to operate safely, efficiently, and sustainably. We strive to blend time-tested engineering principles with forward-thinking innovation, ensuring that every system we design, manufacture, or maintain meets the highest standards of performance and integrity.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We conduct every aspect of our business with honesty, transparency, and accountability — from client partnerships to employee relations.
  • Innovation: We continually invest in research, technology, and process improvement to stay ahead of industry demands and environmental expectations.
  • Quality: Precision and attention to detail are at the core of everything we produce, ensuring lasting reliability for our customers.
  • Safety: We maintain rigorous safety protocols to protect our teams, our clients, and the communities where we operate.
  • Sustainability: Our commitment to responsible manufacturing drives us to reduce waste, optimize energy use, and promote environmentally conscious practices throughout our operations.
  • Collaboration: We believe progress is achieved through teamwork — fostering strong relationships with clients, suppliers, and industry partners.

Together, these values shape the foundation of Markenson Industrials’ continued success and guide our pursuit of excellence across every project, partnership, and innovation.
