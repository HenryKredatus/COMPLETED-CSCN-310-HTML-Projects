About Hammerson Mountain Resort

Founded as a local retreat for mountain lovers, Hammerson Mountain Resort has grown into a destination resort that honors the natural beauty of the area. Our mission is to provide high-quality recreation, hospitality, and community events while preserving the alpine environment.

Our History

Hammerson Mountain Resort began as a humble ski lodge in 1992, nestled in the heart of Wyoming’s Snow Range Mountains. Founded by outdoor enthusiasts who dreamed of creating a year-round alpine retreat, the resort started with just a handful of guest cabins and one modest ski lift.

Over the years, the Hammerson family and their dedicated team transformed the property into a full-service mountain resort without losing its small-town charm. With the addition of fine dining, spa services, and expanded trail systems, the resort grew into a beloved destination for families, adventurers, and travelers seeking peace in the high country.

Today, Hammerson Mountain Resort continues to honor its roots — blending modern comfort with classic mountain hospitality. Each season brings new experiences, from snowy winter slopes to vibrant summer festivals, all while preserving the spirit of the mountains that started it all.
