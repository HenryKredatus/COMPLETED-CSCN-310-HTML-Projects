The Resort

Hammerson Mountain Resort offers comfortable lodges, accessible slopes, and services focused on relaxation and adventure.

Nestled in the heart of the Snowridge Range, the resort combines rustic charm with modern comfort. Whether you're seeking a quiet mountain escape or a basecamp for outdoor thrills, Hammerson Mountain Resort provides year-round opportunities to explore, unwind, and connect with nature. Guests can enjoy ski-in, ski-out convenience during the winter months, and miles of scenic trails for hiking and biking in summer.

Resort Amenities

  • Ski and snowboard rentals
  • Guided hikes and nature walks
  • Spa services and heated pool
  • Conference and event spaces

After a day on the slopes or trails, guests can relax in our full-service spa, featuring mountain mineral treatments and hot stone massages. Dining options range from casual cafés to fine alpine cuisine, each highlighting local ingredients and fresh seasonal flavors. For families, the resort offers year-round programs including ski lessons, stargazing nights, and creative workshops for children.

Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate retreat, Hammerson Mountain Resort offers an unforgettable experience with the beauty of Wyoming’s mountain landscape as your backdrop.
