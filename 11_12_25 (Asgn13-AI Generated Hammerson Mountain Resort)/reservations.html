Room Types

Standard Room

Ideal for solo travelers or couples, our Standard Rooms feature a cozy queen bed, mountain-inspired décor, and a private balcony overlooking the pines.

Deluxe Suite

The Deluxe Suite offers extra space, a king bed, fireplace, and lounge area — perfect for romantic getaways or longer stays in comfort.

Family Suite

Spacious and well-equipped for groups, the Family Suite includes two bedrooms, a full living area, and a kitchenette for convenience and flexibility.
